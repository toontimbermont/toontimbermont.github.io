About This Blog

In this blog, I write articles triggered by questions or conversations I have with inspiring people. Each blog post is typically a more extended answer to something I discussed with them and consider worthwhile to share publicly. Please note that these posts are 100% my own opinions and points of views.

About Me

As a kid, I loved building things.

As a teenager & student, I loved organising things, both at local & international level.

In my twenties & early thirties, I found a passion for IT and worked at Accenture, taking up roles in large projects like tax-on-web and digitisation projects at some big Belgian Banks.

In my thirties & early fourties, I combined my love for building things & IT solutions by founding & growing Skryv from an initial idea to a profitable company with 15 people. Now Skryv is part of the Vanden Broele group, underpinning some of the largest digital public services in Flanders and serving several local governments in Flanders.
