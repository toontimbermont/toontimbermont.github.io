Processing personal data by DATT

DATT is responsible for the processing of personal data. This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for toontimbermont.github.io. It applies solely to information collected by this website.

Cookies

We use “cookies” on this site. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a site visitor’s hard drive. It helps us to improve your access to our site and identify repeat visitors. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on our site.

This site uses Google Analytics which helps us to understand how you use the site and how we can improve your experience. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit so we can continue to produce engaging content. For more information we refer to the Google Analytics Page. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings in your browser. However, this may limit you from using all the features of this website.

Security

At DATT we take security very seriously. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

If you have questions, you can contact us via security@datt.be
